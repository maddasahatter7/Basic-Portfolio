About Me

My name is Madison Barnett. I was born in 1996 in Orlando, FL. When I was 18 I moved to New York to pursue a major in Neuroscience, however, I moved back to Florida two years later after becoming extremely bored. At this time, I changed my major to not only Math but Physics as well. I have always been interested in the Sciences and spend much of my free time reading non-fiction books from this subject. Something else that I thouroughly enjoy is surfing. I love the feeling of the sun on my back and shoulders. The smell and taste of the salt water makes me feel so very alive. There is nothing like surfing. It can change your life if you let it. Surfing has taught me to appreciate nature in a way i've never imagined. This appreciation for nature has led me to be a dedicated animal rights activist and passionate vegan. Animals make my world go 'round.
